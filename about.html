About BlueWave Fish

Founded in 2015, BlueWave Fresh Fish Supplies has been delivering top-quality fish and seafood across Kenya. We partner with trusted local fishermen to ensure freshness, sustainability, and quality in every catch.

Our mission is to make fresh fish accessible to households and restaurants, while supporting local communities and promoting healthy living.
